From the Holy Quran - Surat Al-Fatihah

The virtue of Surat Al-Fatihah.. Surat Al-Fatihah is the greatest Surah in the Holy Qur’an, and it is a Meccan Surah, and it was revealed in order after Surat Al-Muddathir, and it is the first Surah in order in the Qur’an. Almighty: (Praise be to God, Lord of the worlds).
